Audio Reactive Visualizer

A compact audio visualizer. Visuals respond to the currently playing audio.

  • Playback: Start audio with the player Play button.
  • Demo Track: plays the built-in demo song.
  • Choose Track: load a local audio file from your device.
  • Replace Track: replaces the currently loaded local file.
  • Controls: restart, volume, fullscreen, seek and loop are available.
  • Loop mode: enables adjustable start/end markers and a draggable loop region on the timeline.
  • Info: use the player's i button to reopen this panel.
  • Close behavior: clicking outside this panel closes it and clicking outside the player closes the player.
